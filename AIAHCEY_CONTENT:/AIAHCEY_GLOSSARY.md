# AIAHCEY_GLOSSARY.md

## Aiahcey Effect
The feeling fans describe when Aiah and Stacey create a strong impact simply by being together.

## Visual Chemistry
The fandom belief that Aiah and Stacey naturally look good together in photos, performances, and videos.

## The Spark
The electricity fans describe when Aiah and Stacey interact.

## Kikay Duo
A common fandom nickname describing their shared feminine, stylish, and playful energy.

## Matching Energy
The ability to naturally meet each other's vibe, humor, and mood.

## Matching Freak
Fandom phrase describing how they seem to understand each other's weirdness and humor.

## TikTok Bond
The recurring fandom observation that many of their strongest moments appear through TikTok content.

## Comfort Duo
The perception that Aiahcey provides comfort, softness, and familiarity.

## The Pull Effect
The phenomenon where fans enter liking one member and leave attached to both.

## Queen and Princess
A fandom archetype:
- Aiah = Queen
- Stacey = Princess

## Starphrodite
A fan of Aiahcey.
