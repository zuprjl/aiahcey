# AIAHCEY_CANON_V2.md

> Version 2 of the fandom canon, derived from recurring fan observations, reactions, stories, and language.

---

# Core Thesis

Aiahcey is not defined by a single interaction.

Aiahcey is defined by recurring chemistry, shared energy, mutual understanding, visual balance, and a connection that fans repeatedly describe as effortless.

The fandom does not simply observe moments.

The fandom observes patterns.

---

# Visual Chemistry

The strongest recurring fandom theme.

Fans repeatedly describe:

- visual chemistry
- on-stage chemistry
- on-screen chemistry
- bagay sila
- sparks
- chemistry is insane

Many fans believe that Aiah and Stacey create impact simply by being placed in the same frame.

The pairing is frequently described as aesthetically balanced and visually complementary.

---

# The Spark

A recurring fandom word.

Fans often describe:

- sparks
- electricity
- tension
- energy

The concept of "spark" is treated separately from chemistry.

Chemistry is compatibility.

Spark is impact.

Fans often believe Aiahcey possesses both.
